Life in Wuxi

Clothing

Wuxi is known for its silk production, with a history of over 1,400 years. The city is famous for its high-quality silk products, including garments, scarves, and ties.

Food

Wuxi is renowned for its freshwater seafood, especially the "Sister-in-law's Fish" and "Taihu Lake Crabs". The city's local cuisine features sweet flavors, such as "Hongshan Beancurd" and "Sugared Lotus Roots".

Housing

Wuxi has a mix of modern and traditional housing. The city center has high-rise apartments and hotels, while the outskirts offer more affordable options, such as courtyard houses and villas.

Transportation

Wuxi has an extensive public transportation system, including buses, subways, and taxis. It also has a bike-sharing program and scenic cycling routes along Taihu Lake. Wuxi is also home to the world's first commercial maglev train.
