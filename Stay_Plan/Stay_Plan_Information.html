BASIC INFORMATION

Twitter FacebookEmail

Wuxi, a vibrant city in Jiangsu Province, China, covers an area of 4,787 square kilometers and has a population of approximately 6.3 million. Located on the banks of the majestic Taihu Lake, Wuxi is often referred to as the "Pearl of Taihu Lake." The city is well-connected to major urban centers through its extensive transportation network, including highways, railways, and an airport. Wuxi is renowned for its rich history, unique culture, and picturesque landscapes. With thriving industries such as manufacturing, high-tech, and modern services, Wuxi offers ample work opportunities for its residents and has become an attractive destination for both tourists and professionals.
