COMPANIES

Twitter FacebookEmail

The top-5 companies in Wuxi excel in diverse fields, including pharmaceuticals and medical devices (Wuxi AppTec), solar energy (Wuxi Suntech Power), industrial manufacturing (Wuxi Taiji Industry), household appliances (Wuxi Little Swan), and intelligent equipment for renewable energy (Wuxi Lead Intelligent Equipment).

Wuxi AppTec


Wuxi AppTec is a leading pharmaceutical and medical device company, offering integrated services in drug research, discovery, development, and manufacturing, as well as medical device testing and genomics, with operations in China, the United States, and Europe.


Wuxi Suntech Power Co., Ltd.


Wuxi Suntech Power specializes in solar energy, producing high-quality solar cells, modules, and photovoltaic systems. The company focuses on innovation, quality, and customer service to make solar energy more accessible and affordable worldwide.


Wuxi Taiji Industry Co., Ltd.

Wuxi Taiji Industry is a diversified industrial company, manufacturing and developing pressure vessels, environmental protection equipment, and energy-saving technologies. The company serves the chemical, petrochemical, pharmaceutical, and energy industries, emphasizing sustainable development.


Wuxi Lead Intelligent Equipment Co., Ltd.

Wuxi Lead Intelligent Equipment is a high-tech enterprise specializing in lithium-ion battery manufacturing equipment, solar cell production equipment, and automation solutions for various industries, focusing on technological innovation, reliability, and efficiency.


Wuxi Little Swan Co., Ltd.

Wuxi Little Swan produces household appliances, particularly washing machines, and is known for innovation and quality in the domestic appliance market. The company aims to improve customers' quality of life through energy-efficient, user-friendly products.
