On June 1, 2022, the Business Charm Ranking of 2022 Cities was officially released. Among 337 cities at prefecture level or above in mainland China evaluated in the list, the provincial capital of Changsha remained in the top 10, followed by 15 new first-tier cities: Chengdu, Chongqing, Hangzhou, Wuxi, Wuhan, Suzhou, Zhengzhou, Nanjing, Tianjin, Changsha, Dongguan, Ningbo, Foshan, Hefei and Qingdao.

From the statistics, the GDP of Wuxi reached 1.49 trillion yuan in 2022, with a year-on-year growth of 6.05%. In comparison, the GDP growth rate of Wuxi from 2011 to 2022 basically maintained above the national GDP growth rate and the average growth rate of new first-tier cities, with relatively good development.

Growth rate of fixed assets investment in Wuxi City

From 2017 to 2022, the national total fixed asset investment showed a downward trend, and the total fixed asset investment in new first-tier cities also showed a downward trend. The growth rate of Wuxi's fixed asset fluctuated around the national average level, and the growth rate of Wuxi's fixed asset investment in 2022 was higher than the national level and the average growth rate of new first-tier cities.


The export of goods of Wuxi City

In general, from 2011 to 2022, the export volume of goods in Wuxi showed a fluctuating upward trend. Compared with the growth rate of the national export of goods and the average growth rate of new first-tier cities, the export growth rate of Wuxi from 2011 to 2022 is relatively consistent with the change trend of the national average level, and the cyclical change trend of the growth rate of the export of goods is obvious.

Retail sales of consumer goods in Wuxi

The retail sales of consumer goods in Wuxi showed an upward trend from 2011 to 2019, and decreased in 2020 due to the epidemic.The change trend of retail sales of consumer goods in Wuxi during 2011-2022 is basically consistent with the national year-on-year growth rate and the average growth rate of new first-tier cities.

Tax revenue of each district

According to the statistical bulletin of each district and county in 2022, the average tax revenue of each district in Wuxi in 2022 is 13.981 billion yuan, and there are two urban areas with higher than the average tax revenue, among which Xinwu District has the largest tax revenue scale, reaching 24.206 billion yuan in 2022, followed by Jiangyin City with 22.683 billion yuan in 2022..


Changes in investment by district

According to the 2022 local economic bulletin, Yixing city saw the fastest growth of 10.9 percent in fixed asset investment in 2022, followed by Liangxi district, which saw a 10 percent growth in fixed asset investment in 2022. At the same time, the growth rate offixed asset investment in Xishan District of Wuxi City also exceeded 5% in 2022.
